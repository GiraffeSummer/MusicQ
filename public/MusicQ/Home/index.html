MusicQ

MusicQ is an app that allows users across multiple devices with internet access to add videos from YouTube on a single playlist.
This is great for parties, as people can add music from their own phone onto the playlist.


Quick-start Guide

You can easily get started with MusicQ in a few steps:

Step 1: Click on the button "Quick Start" or either of the buttons above.
Step 2: Create a room, Enter a name that will help people find it
  -Playlists: Enter a Youtube Playlist ID or URL into the box, and click "Check Playlist" to verify if it works
  -Optional: Enter a password and Playlist URL or ID
Step 3: Let people add their own music/videos to the Queue by letting them find the room in the list, or scan the QR code
Step 4: Click on the QR-Code to add songs yourself from the same device

  -Best usage: Put the player on a laptop or computer connected to some kind of speaker(s)
